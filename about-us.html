Our Story

FLUFFY PHOTOGRAPHY is a ontario-based pet photography company. Our fonder Sophie Lu have one dog that company with her for many years, but in her yong age taking photo is not as easy as now, so she didn’t have many photo of her fluffy family member. Therefore, she create this company to help people that want to record their moment with their fluffy family member.

Our Mission

FLUFFY PHOTOGRAPHY try hard to reach every client’s objective, and use camera to record the memories between people and their adorable fluffy family. Our goal is to make everyone smile when they look at those photo, and they will remeber the good time they have with their fluffy partner!
